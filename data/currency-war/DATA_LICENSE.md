The enriched Currency War dataset includes material collected from:

- HoYoverse / miHoYo official Currency War announcements and game documentation.

BWIKI pages state that their community content is provided under
[CC BY-NC-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/).
The checked-in derived data must therefore retain attribution, remain
non-commercial, and follow the share-alike requirement where that license
applies.
